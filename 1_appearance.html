Appearance

The first step is to assess the wine’s color and clarity.

With 1 to 2 ounces of wine in the glass, place the glass on a sheet of plain white paper. From above, look down the glass while noting the wine’s clarity. Next tilt the glass on an angle and note the color shade of the wine, while paying attention to the rim of the wine.
