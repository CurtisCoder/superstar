Body

After swallowing or spitting the wine, you can now complete your evaluation.

The next stage is to note the body or weight of the wine. The body is determined by its alcoholic strength and to a much lesser extent, the amount of residual sugar and extract (dissolved solids).

A general guideline of descriptors regarding body and alcohol levels follows.

Light - below 10%

Medium - 10% to 12%

Full - 13% and up
